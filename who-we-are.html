Who We Are

Meet the hearts behind the movement

Our Story

Safa Hearts began with a simple belief: compassion is one of the most powerful forces for positive change. Founded by someone who once needed help himself, Safa Hearts grew out of the support and generosity he experienced. Inspired by the Islamic principle of sadaqah — voluntary acts of charity and kindness — we work to make giving and caring a way of life for everyone. While our values are rooted in a rich tradition of service, our doors are open to all. We welcome every heart that wishes to help another, regardless of faith or background. Together we can lift lives, ease hardship, and spread hope.

Every day, we witness the ripple of kindness spreading through communities. One act of help sparks another, creating a chain of goodwill that touches countless lives. At Safa Hearts, we believe every small gesture matters, and every heart has the power to make a difference.

Our Team

Ryan Burns, Founder & CEO

Right now, it’s just me — but change doesn’t need a crowd. Inspiration often starts with one heart willing to act. My journey began at home, noticing the power of small acts of kindness. I extended care to my household, my close relatives, and then to those in need — travelers, neighbors, and distant communities. It all began with a simple smile, my first step toward purpose. I didn’t have money at the time (and I still don’t), so I gave what I could: my time, my effort, my attention. I helped without expectation or recognition, finding contentment in giving for the sake of Allah. By no means is there perfection — I strive to grow, to learn, and to act with sincerity at every opportunity. Safa Hearts exists to show that even the smallest act, done with intention, can ripple out to touch countless lives.
